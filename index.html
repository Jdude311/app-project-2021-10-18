Video Game Categories

There are many different types of video games, and this may be confusing at first. This guide will help you understand them and find a video game you'll love.

Shooter Games

Shooter games are some of the most popular and prevalent games today. They're an abolute...blast...if shooters are your kind of thing. Check out some shooter games here.

Engineering/Automation Games

Often described as incredibly satisfying (while also being "thinking" games), in engineering or automation games, you will create a system--maybe to build a factory, or to create a plane--that, when working, will give you the ultimate sense of satisfaction. Check out some more engineering games here.

Social Games

These games are best played with friends and can be an incredibly fun experience. They became especially popular during lockdown, when people couldn't hang out with their friends in person. Check them out here.

Role Playing Games (RPGs)

Role playing games are a bit like Dungeons & Dragons, but in a video game. They're characterized by strong world building and storytelling. You can find more about RPGs here.
