Social Games

Social games are games that are collaberative and requrire multiple people to play. These games tend to be a way for friends to connect to eachother online in a fun and enguaging way.

Among Us

This is a perfect example of a social game because the whole premise of the game is that there is a group of people who are on a spaceship required to do tasks to fix the ship. But, in this group of people there are "imposters" who sabatage the ship and try to eliminate as many people as possible before the tasks are completed. Additionally, in the game when someone calls a meeting the various people have to guess who the imposter is and kick them off the ship. To download it on Google Play, click here.

Animal Crossing

This game is a simulated community where people play as wacky and whimsical humaniod animals. In this game people can do activites such as fishing, catching bugs, watering plants, planting trees, and aquiring items to decorate their homes.

This game has actully been known as a good escape for people with anxiety. Also, it helps them enguage in socioty without triggering any of their symtoms.
